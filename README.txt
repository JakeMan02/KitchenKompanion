Instructions for starting up our Kitchen Kompanion App

(1) Unzip folder to desired location on your computer (Desktop is suggested as it is easy to access)

(2) Open folder

(3) Launch the home.html file in your desired browser

(4) Right click in browser and select "Inspect Element"

(5) Click the gear in the top right of the page to access settings

(6) Navigate to Devices

(7) Click "Add custom device..."

(8) Name the Device whatever you'd like and set the dimensions to 640x960 from 400x700

(9) Click "Add"

(10) At the top of the screen, above the app, you should see a drop down box. Click the drop down box and select your newly created device

**If the app is zoomed in after doing this. Click somewhere on the app and then press Ctrl+0
